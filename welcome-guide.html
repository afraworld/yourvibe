Things to know upfront

Simple facts about how this space works. Read once, no surprises.

Can I save or share content?
No. Saving, screenshotting, or sharing in any form results in immediate permanent removal with no refund.
What happens when I cancel?
Access stops at end of the current period. Archive pauses too. Resubscribe anytime — everything returns immediately.
I missed a month. Can I get that content?
Yes. Past months are packaged and available for individual purchase. Ask in the bot for what is available. One-time purchase — yours to keep.
Is the Friday live recorded?
No. Live only. It goes into the monthly archive when the month closes. Want it permanently — you need Archive Access.
How do custom requests work?
Paid Chat only. I decide if I agree. If yes — I price it, you pay before I create anything.
Can I get Archive without Weekly?
Individual monthly packs — yes. Full archive channel — only with active Weekly subscription.
What time is the Friday live?
Every Friday. It is not rescheduled.
