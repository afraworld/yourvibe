Before you join

Things to know first

Simple. Read once.

Can I save or share content?
No. Recordings are private and personal. Saving, sharing, or redistributing in any form means immediate permanent removal. No exceptions, no refund.
Is Archive linked to Weekly?
No. Monthly Archive is completely independent. You can subscribe to Archive without ever joining Weekly. Both run separately.
What happens when I cancel?
Access stops at the end of the current period. Resubscribe anytime and access returns immediately.
Are ASMR stories recorded?
They are posted live and disappear after 24 hours. They do not go into the archive. The Saturday voice drop is what gets archived.
How do I request a custom recording?
Through Paid Chat only. I decide whether I agree. If yes — I name the price, you pay before I record. No exceptions to this order.
